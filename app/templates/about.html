Our Mission

One Community, One Voice, One Government

We believe that democracy works best when citizens are informed, engaged, and heard. CivicLens was built to create unity between the community, their voice, and the government — making it easier for residents of Hidalgo County to participate in the decisions that shape their lives.

What We Do

Technology for Civic Good

📡

Track Hearings

We automatically pull Hidalgo County government hearings from YouTube so you never miss what your elected officials are deciding.

🤖

AI Summaries

Complex government hearings are summarized by AI into plain English — so everyone can understand what's being decided, not just lawyers.

📊

Accountability

We compare government decisions to public comments and score them — aligned, partial, or diverged — so you know if your voice was heard.
